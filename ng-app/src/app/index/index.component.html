Welcome to Better Polling This site was created to help users overcome the issues present in first-past-the-post voting, the voting method prevalent in internet polls and American politics. Develop a better consensus using our polls, whether you need to choose a restaurant among friends or decide the president of your organization.
Methodology Currently, our platform will attempt to determine a Condorcet winner. If there is none, we will return the winner of an instant runoff .

We hope to expand into more sophisticated methods in the near future and allow pollers to choose their preferred methods.
